Skill

Communication

- This refers to the ability to convey information effectively and efficiently, both verbally and in writing. Strong communication skills involve active listening, clear articulation of thoughts, and the ability to empathize with others. These skills are essential in both personal and professional settings.

Problem Solving

This skill involves the ability to analyze a situation, identify problems, and come up with effective solutions. It includes critical thinking, creativity, and decision-making abilities. Those with strong problem-solving skills can navigate challenges and make sound decisions based on available information.
